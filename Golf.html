Golf

Golf is a precision sport played outdoors, where players aim to get a ball into each hole in the fewest number of shots.

Introduction of Golf

Golf is an outdoor sport played on a large course, where players use clubs to hit a small ball into a series of holes in as few strokes as possible. It is known for its calm atmosphere, strategic play, and beautiful landscapes.

How Many Players Play the Game?

Golf is usually played by 1 to 4 players in a group, but professional tournaments can include hundreds of individual players competing separately.

Is it a team game?

Golf is primarily an individual sport, but it can also be played in teams during special formats like Ryder Cup or team tournaments, where combined scores matter.

Rule of the game

Play the Ball as It Lies

You must play the ball from where it lands‚Äîno moving or touching it (unless allowed by specific rules).

Tee Off Within the Tee Box

Every hole starts from a designated tee area; your ball must be teed between and not in front of the markers.

Count Every Stroke

Each swing made with the intent to hit the ball counts as a stroke, including penalty strokes

Out of Bounds & Penalties

If your ball goes out of bounds or into water, you take a penalty stroke and drop a new ball.

Putting on the Green

Once on the green, players use a putter to gently roll the ball into the hole.

Honesty & Etiquette

Golf relies on player honesty for scoring and respect for others by keeping quiet and not distracting during shots.

Important Facts About Golf

Ancient Origins

Golf originated in Scotland during the 15th century and is now played worldwide.

18-Hole Standard

A full round of golf usually consists of 18 holes, though 9-hole courses are also common.

No Standard Playing Field

Unlike other sports, every golf course is different, with unique layouts, lengths, and obstacles.

The Ball is Not Moved

Golf is one of the few sports where the ball is not moved by an opponent‚Äîonly the player's own shots count.

Olympic Sport

Golf was reintroduced into the Olympic Games in 2016 after being absent for over a century.

Popular Global Sport

Golf is played by millions of people around the world, both professionally and recreationally.
